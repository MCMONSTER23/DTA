Three Light Foundation

Three Light Foundation is the foundation that was founded by IRADUKUNDA Arrhenius, aiming to unite people globally to make positive changes, particularly for those facing disabilities, mental health challenges and taking back to school children from poor families through volunteering. The most involvers are young generation who are willing to provide positive impact. In partnership with Three Light Foundation, Day Tour Agency give the hand to foundation in those activities of providing support to the community, with the target to make positive impact in the society. Also tries to prepare some tours for youth like hiking with the foundation, aiming environment conservation. Its open to anyone who may need to participate within those activities of charity, together with both Day Tour Agency and Three Light Foundation.
